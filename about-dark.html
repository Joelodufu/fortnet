About Fortnet

Driving Logistics Excellence Across Nigeria

Who We Are

Fortnet is more than logistics. We are Nigeria’s trusted partner for supply chain excellence, ensuring safe, timely, and cost-effective delivery across 30+ states. With over 5 years of experience, our solutions power both public and private sectors, meeting global standards with local expertise.

Our Story

Founded on innovation, professionalism, and customer trust, Fortnet has supported projects with USAID, the Global Fund, the Federal Ministry of Health, and Chemonics. We remain committed to redefining logistics in Nigeria with international best practices.

Our Vision

To be Nigeria’s most trusted logistics partner, enabling seamless flow of goods and services nationwide.

Our Mission

Deliver reliable, efficient, and innovative logistics solutions while building sustainable partnerships.
